Our Co-Founders:

Jordyn (Hor-Deen) Davies

Jordyn, or as her friends call her, "Hor-deen", is a beacon positivity to those around her. Jordyn is excited to share her excess love with others in her new self-love club.

Claire Kelly

Claire Kelly practices self-love everyday through small acts of positivity, but felt like she needed more. Claire co-founded the self-love club at Ida B. Wells in an attempt to share her knowledge of self-love with a bigger audience.

Cooper Kozitza

Cooper isn't really sure what self-love is, but he does love the idea of putting it on his college aplications. Cooper hopes to learn more about self-love by assisting in the creation of this club.

Avila Hurd

Avila's number one goal in life is to educate people on the importance of self-love. By co-founding the self-love club, she can finally acheive her dreams, and share her inspiring story of self-love.
