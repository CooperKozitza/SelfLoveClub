Welcome to Self-Love Club

The Self Love Club aims to promote a culture of positivity, acceptance, and love for one's self and other members of our community. Join us for monthly meetings taking place on the first Tuesday of the month to learn more about self love and emotional well-being.

About Us

Our Mission:

Our mission is to empower and support students at Ida B. Wells to love and accept themselves for who they are by providing a safe and inclusive space for self-discovery, and promoting mental and emotional well-being through education and community-building activities.

Media and Organizations:

We The Urban

WE THE URBAN exists to enlighten, inspire, and empower today and tomorrow's cultural pacesetters through galvanizing, forward-thinking visuals and fearless storytelling.

Self Love Club loves We The Urban's message, and their constant commitment to sharing and promoting self love. They publish bi-weekly stories by "multicultural vanguard creatives who push boundaries in fashion, art, music, and various other respective creative fields."

Here at Ida B. Wells:

Other Clubs

Many other clubs at Ida B. Wells share similar values with the Self Love Club, like making a positive impact on your community and promoting mental health awareness. Below are some examples of clubs you should try out:

Positive Charge

Thursdays at Lunch in the Auditorium Foyer (Starting September 29th)

Positive Charge! PDX Club is an IBW student club affiliate of the nonprofit Positive Charge! PDX, which strives to amplify kindness. We participate in easy, meaningful volunteer opportunities that help our community here in Portland, across the state and sometimes across borders. When that happens, kindness isn't just expressed. It's amplified!

Key Club

Thursdays at Lunch in Room 106

Key Club Members around the world are learning how to lead and stand up for what's right through service and volunteerism. In partnership with the local Kiwanis Club, IBWHS students are making a positive impact as they serve others in the school and community.

Students Active for Ending Rape (SAFER)

1st and 3rd Wednesdays During Lunch in Room 35 (Starting October 5th)

Our mission is to educate and raise awareness about sexual assault that take place on our campus and in our community.
